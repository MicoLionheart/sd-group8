Age Pension: Secure Your Retirement with Financial Stability

Age Pension is a government-provided financial support system designed to help retirees maintain their quality of life. Understanding eligibility criteria and benefits can ensure a stress-free retirement.

Who is Eligible for Age Pension?

  • Age Requirement – Must meet the minimum pension age set by the government.
  • Residency Conditions – Must be an Australian resident for a specific period.
  • Income and Asset Test – Must meet financial limits to qualify.
